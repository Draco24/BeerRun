BeerRun

by Kyle Racette

WHAT DO??

You've been invited to the party of the century! There's music, games, dancing, booze... but oh wait... someone's gotta bring that last one. Too bad, looks like you drew the short straw here buddy... we need you to go out and get us some BEER if you wanna come to the party. Oh yea, and we recommend you maintain a healthy buzz. Good luck!

CONTROLS:

  • Movement - arrows or WASD
  • Drink a beer - space bar
